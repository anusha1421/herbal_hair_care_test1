Description

This graph illustrates the progression of hair loss, allowing us to identify the different stages of hair loss. Here's what each stage represents:


  • Stage 0: Normal hair condition.

  • Stage 1: Minimal hair fall.

  • Stages 2 and 3: Noticeable hair loss.

  • Stage 4: Complete baldness.

When you upload an image, the graph indicates the corresponding stage of hair loss and shows the dates when you uploaded it. If you check your hair status weekly or daily, you may observe fluctuations between stages 0 or 1 and others. If the line remains on the same stage, it suggests that your hair loss stage is similar to the previous check. Consider continuing the recommended treatment for an additional 3 months to assess any changes effectively.
